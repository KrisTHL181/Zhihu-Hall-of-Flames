This journal gathers outstanding researchers across various fields who perform multiple rounds of mental simulation experiments using highly condensed knowledge structures and superior imaginative reasoning. A manuscript passes peer review if at least one reviewer deems it worthy. If multiple reviewers rate the quality as exceptional, it is categorized as a **Good Entry**.
* **Principle of Simplicity Priority**: We do not need complex formulas or tongue-twisting theorems; we only require concise analysis. True truth is never complex. The more grand a theory, the more it should be resolved in three lines; the more profound a problem, the more it should be solved after a single dinner‚Äôs reflection. * **Preference for Analogical Completeness**: A qualified theory should integrate the universe, consciousness, energy conservation, stock market fluctuations, and more into a single theoretical framework. Theories that generalize traditional Chinese science or personal empirical experiences into fundamental models of cosmic operation are considered highly rational.

## üìù Submission Guide

To maintain the academic purity and epic tension of the Hall of Flames, please strictly follow the submission process. If you cannot access GitHub due to network issues, please contact me via Zhihu private message.

### 1. Submission Methods

#### I. GitHub Issues

Create a new Issue in this repository. Suggested title format:

> [Field] + [Core Goal of Theory]: (Optional) Core argument of the article.

Example:

> [Mathematics] I proved the Riemann Hypothesis: There is a close connection between the Mandelbrot set and the Metallic Mean; the self-similar structure of prime distribution is completely isomorphic to the fractal structure of the Mandelbrot set near metallic points.

#### II. Zhihu Private Message

If you cannot complete the GitHub submission process, contact any [professional reviewer](https://github.com/KrisTHL181/Zhihu-Hall-of-Flames/graphs/contributors). If you cannot open that link, contact [me personally](https://www.zhihu.com/people/KrisTHL181). Content should follow the same structured format as an Issue for efficient pre-review.

### 2. Required/Recommended Elements

**Must include:**

* An **accessible** link to the original text or a Wayback Machine backup.
* **Author‚Äôs profile or contact info**. If the post is a repost, provide the *original author‚Äôs* ID or source.

**Optional but recommended:**

* A concise summary of the article. (Note: Without this, the review process may be significantly delayed).
* Your reasons for recommending it to the Hall of Flames.
* Improvements over existing theories or key highlights.

**Please note:**

1. Private message submissions are "offline channels" and do not lower review standards.
2. Reviewers may not reply immediately. A lack of instant response does not mean the theory is disqualified.
3. We may **reformat or organize your manuscript**. If your draft has minor issues like colloquialisms or formatting errors, we may revise it for you rather than rejecting it.

### 3. Post-Acceptance Process

Once accepted, manuscripts are archived using a unified template. Theories are categorized as Good or Featured based on their quality. We may attach brief structural commentary to preserve the original "intellectual tension." For contributors willing to add the maintainer on WeChat, rewards of 5‚Äì20 RMB may be provided depending on the research quality. **Note: Generally, only the lead maintainer ([myself](https://www.zhihu.com/people/KrisTHL181)) provides monetary rewards.**

### üåü Featured Entries

**Featured Entries** are theoretical works that we believe profoundly reveal the truth, completely overturn existing scientific paradigms, and serve as stellar examples of independent scientific thought. To become a "Featured" entry, a theory must transcend simple whimsy; *it must reach a state of becoming a brand-new field of study.* It is not just a challenge to old knowledge systems, but a bold and elegant reconstruction of truth.

A qualified Featured Entry typically possesses the following traits (though it need not meet all):

* **Epic Disruption**: The core argument must be able to overturn at least one fundamental theory widely accepted by mainstream science or establish a new discipline in a brief description.
* **Unparalleled Simplicity and Self-Consistency**: True cosmic truths are never complicated. Featured entries often explain almost all daily phenomena through a simple formula or ingenious analogy, showing incredible unity.
* **Profound Original Spirit**: These are the crystallizations of independent thought, often originating from a sudden epiphany or deep insight into the nature of science, carrying a strong personal style.

### üëç Good Entries

**Good Entries** are academic achievements that are logically self-consistent and provide significant value, even if they do not reach the "Featured" level. These are selected through honorary evaluation by one or more reviewers.

Simply put, they are written with high **impact**: reasonable logic, valuable information, broad coverage, and refined content. Where possible, they are accompanied by **theoretical sketches** or **photographic evidence**.
